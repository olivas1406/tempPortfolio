About Me

I've been working with technology in some capacity for the last 19 years, most recently as a Network Consultant, but I am now transitioning into a new role as a Full Stack Developer. My extensive work experience includes everything from troubleshooting electronic missile guidance systems for Raytheon to Windows server support and network monitoring to consulting on organizational Security Posture. I previously held the CCNA Routing & Switching and CCNA Security certifications, though both expired in Feburuary 2018 . When I'm not providing free tech support to family and friends, I'm usually spending time with my two daughters.
